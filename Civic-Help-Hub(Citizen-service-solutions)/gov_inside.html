Certificate Application Guidance

Birth Certificate
  1. Submit Form A with parent's ID proof and hospital birth record
  2. Verification process takes 3-5 working days
  3. Certificate can be collected in person or delivered via post
Residence Certificate
  1. Submit address proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, or Utility Bill)
  2. Verification by local administration officer
  3. Processing time: 7-10 working days
Income Certificate
  1. Fill Form B-12 with last 3 months salary slips
  2. Attach bank statements and employer certificate
  3. Certificate issued within 10 working days
Marriage Certificate
  1. Submit marriage registration form with witnesses
  2. Provide photo ID of both partners
  3. Certificate issued on the same day after verification
