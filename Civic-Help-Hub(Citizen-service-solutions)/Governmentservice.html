Government Services Assistance

Get help with complex government procedures from verified volunteers

Service Details

1.Smart Card Applications

Requirements:

  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
  • Proof of Address
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Completed application form

Process Steps:

  1. Fill out the application form with correct details
  2. Attach all required documents
  3. Submit at the designated government office
  4. Track application status
  5. Collect the smart card when ready

2.Passport Assistance

Requirements:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID or PAN Card
  • Birth Certificate
  • Address Proof
  • Passport-sized photographs with white background

3.PAN CARD Assistance

Requirements:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Individual Phone Number
  • Birth Certificate
  • Address Proof
  • Scanned Signature
  • Passport-sized photographs with white background(Allocated Size)
